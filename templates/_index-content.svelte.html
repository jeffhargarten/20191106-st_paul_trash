East Side precincts were an island of opposition
Voters in St. Paul gave decisive approval to the city's year-old system of organized trash collection, despite a spirited campaign by opponents to jettison a service that they costs too much and imposes too many rules. Except for a swath of north and east St. Paul, and a few other places, yes voters outnumbered no voters across the city.
